How to Play

  • Click on aircraft or datatag to select them (they turn yellow)
  • Click datatag multiple times to rotate it around the aircraft (top-right → top-left → bottom-left → bottom-right)
  • Drag the command textbox by clicking and dragging the title bar to move it anywhere on screen
  • Use Mouse Wheel to zoom in/out on the radar display
  • Press H to issue a heading command (0-359 degrees)
  • Press A to issue an altitude command (in hundreds of feet, e.g., 100 = 10,000 ft)
  • Press S to issue a speed command (knots)
  • Press D to issue a direct-to command (type waypoint name like TORAZ, CATUZ, ATICO, NICRA)
  • Type numbers/letters and press ENTER to confirm
  • Press ESC to deselect aircraft or cancel command

Objective

  • Maintain safe separation: 5 nautical miles horizontally OR 1000 feet vertically
  • Violations will reduce your score
  • Guide arrivals to the airport and departures away safely

Display

  • Green triangles: Aircraft pointing in their heading direction
  • Yellow triangles: Selected aircraft
  • Semi-transparent lines behind aircraft: Heading indicators showing direction of travel (15nm trail)
  • Data tags show: Callsign, Aircraft Type, Altitude (hundreds of feet), Speed (knots)
  • Radial lines from center: Compass headings every 30° with labels (000, 030, 060, etc.)
  • Cyan triangles: IAF waypoints (Initial Approach Fixes)
  • White circle with crosshair: Airport
  • Bright gray lines: Runways with numbers
  • Orange/Red lines: Separation conflicts (orange = warning, red = critical)
  • Concentric circles: Distance rings (every 10 nautical miles)
